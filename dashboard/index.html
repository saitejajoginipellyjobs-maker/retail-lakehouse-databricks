Medallion Architecture

🥉 Bronze Layer

Raw ingestion with schema enforcement. No transformation. Full audit trail.

  • raw_sales
  • raw_orders
  • raw_inventory
→

🥈 Silver Layer

Cleansed, deduped, type-cast and enriched data. SCD-1 upserts via Delta MERGE.

  • sales (with revenue, margins, date dims)
  • orders (with days_to_ship)
  • inventory (with low_stock flag)
→

🥇 Gold Layer

Business-ready aggregations consumed by dashboards and BI tools.

  • daily_sales_summary
  • product_performance
  • weekly_trends
